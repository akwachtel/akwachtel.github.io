About CommonGround Communications

Founded in 2017, CommonGround Communications specializes in meaningful strategic communications engagements, trainings and products for clients determined to impact their local and national communities. We work with nonprofits, foundations, and governmental agencies to develop strategic communication initiatives and social cause campaigns.
