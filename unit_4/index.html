About CommonGround Communications

Founded in 2017, CommonGround Communications specializes in meaningful strategic communications engagements, trainings and products for clients determined to impact their local and national communities. Udeitha Srimushnam, founder of CommonGround Communications, has developed strategic communication initiatives with a focus on social cause campaigns with nonprofits, foundations and governmental agencies for 5 years.

Udeitha brings passion and expertise to all her clients, which have included the National Parks Service, The California Endowment, The Center for Western Priorities, and many more. She has developed expertise in advocacy communications, developing, deploying and measuring successful communications campaigns in education advocacy in more than 10 states. She has helped to launch several education nonprofits from soup to nuts and has advised on organizational branding and messaging, social media, website, earned media, speaker training, media training, crisis communications and student enrollment strategies.

Udeitha has served as an Senior Account Associate at RALLY (formerly Griffin|Schein), a social cause campaign and communications firm in Los Angeles, as well as Communications Manager for 50CAN: The 50 State Campaign for Achievement Now, a nonprofit network of local leaders advocating for a high-quality education for all kids. She has held strategic communications positions with the National Parks Service and Forbes Marshall and has presented on her strategic communications work for the International Association of Business Communicators at the 2017 International Communication Management Conference.
